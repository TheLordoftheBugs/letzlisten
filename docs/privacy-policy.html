Privacy Policy

App: LetzListen
Last updated: February 2026

Overview

LetzListen is a radio streaming application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data (if any) we collect and how it is used.

Data We Do Not Collect

LetzListen does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account or registration is required
  • No name, email address, or contact information is collected
  • No location data is accessed or stored
  • No usage analytics or tracking is performed
  • No advertising identifiers are used

Internet Connection

The app requires an internet connection solely to stream radio stations. All streams are publicly available radio broadcasts. We do not log or store information about which stations you listen to.

Third-Party Services

LetzListen does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, the new version will be posted at this URL with an updated date.
