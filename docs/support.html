Frequently Asked Questions

The radio stream is not playing. What should I do?
Make sure you have an active internet connection. Some streams may be temporarily unavailable — try again later or select a different station.

The sound cuts out or buffers frequently.
This is usually caused by a slow or unstable internet connection. Try switching to Wi-Fi or moving to an area with better signal.

A station is missing or not working.
Radio stream URLs can change over time. Please contact us with the station name and we'll update it as soon as possible.

The app crashes or behaves unexpectedly.
Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, please contact us with a description of what happened.
